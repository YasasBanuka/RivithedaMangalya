Join us in celebrating Aluth Avurudda, the Sinhalese New Year—a time of renewal, gratitude, and community. The University of Vocational Technology invites you to participate in our traditional festivities honoring Sri Lankan heritage through cultural performances, traditional games, and authentic cuisine. This celebration marks the sun's movement from Meena Rashiya (House of Pisces) to Mesha Rashiya (House of Aries).
